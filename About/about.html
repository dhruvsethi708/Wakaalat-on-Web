Our Vision

Hillwoods is a completely secular institution that instills into its students a deep regard for Indian traditions and customs. Children are free to practise any religion of their choice, and the school celebrates all major festivals with equal joy and reverence. Prayers from different religions are chanted on occasions like Gandhi Jayanti. The school believes in exposing children to diverse faiths and beliefs so that they may become responsible, cultured and dedicated individuals. It is therefore the primary aim of Hillwoodians to strive to bring out the best in themselves, not only in academics and sports arena, but more so in their character. Since children spend their formative years in school, the environment in which they grow up is of tremendous significance. At Hillwoods we seek to develop a sense of values and strength , committment and devotion in each student, and we seek to create an atmosphere of honesty, of intellectual curiosity essential to the unfettered blossoming of a young mind.
